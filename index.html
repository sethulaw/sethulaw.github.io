About

Sethu Law Firm, established by Sidhartha Sethupathy, embodies a client-centric ethos, prioritizing individualized legal solutions. Their approach harmonizes expertise with a deep understanding of client needs, fostering trust and tailored strategies. Through personalized attention and a commitment to client objectives, Sethu Law navigates complexities with finesse, ensuring comprehensive and empathetic legal counsel. Their hallmark lies in dedicated advocacy and a steadfast focus on client empowerment within the legal realm.

Sidhartha Sethupathy is a distinguished lawyer and advocate renowned for his expertise in criminal, civil, and divorce law. His proficiency extends to wills, documentation, land registration, licensing, and service and labor writ.
